Top three cultures in Nigeria

The Yoruba Culture Group

Yoruba, one of the three largest ethnic groups of Nigeria, concentrated in the southwestern part of the country. The Yoruba numbered more than 20 million at the turn of the 21st century.

The Igbo Culture Group

Igbo, also called Ibo, people living chiefly in southeastern Nigeria who speak Igbo, a language of the Benue-Congo branch of the Niger-Congo language family.

The Hausa Culture Group

Hausa, people found chiefly in northwestern Nigeria and adjacent southern Niger. They constitute the largest ethnic group in the area, which also contains another large group, like the Fulani.
